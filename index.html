DO NGOC CUONG - GAME DESIGNER PORTFOLIO

DO NGOC CUONG

- GAME DESIGNER -

Hello, my name is Do Ngoc Cuong (Vietnamese: Đỗ Ngọc Cường). I am currently working as an independent Game Developer and Game Designer.

Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from University of Information Technology - Vietnam National University, Ho Chi Minh City (UIT - VNUHCM).

Specializing in Combat Design and Level Design, I contributed to the release of multiple successful titles when working at VNG and Brave Stars Studio.

Below is my portfolio, highlighting my work and experience as a professional Game Designer.

Projects:

Bag Master Isekai

My most recent project, "Bag Master Isekai" introduces a new take on bag-management RPGs, with new and unique UX enhancements compared to other bag-management games. Players assume the role of a hero summoned to a fantasy world, and using a magical bag to collect weapons in order to fight hordes of enemies defending the kingdom ruled by Baron Bacon.

Genre: Rougelite, Action-Adventure, Strategy
Engine: Unity
Team Size: 5 members
Platform: Mobile (Android)
Duration: January 2025 - Present
Achievements: Promissing beta retention result (~60% RR1, ~15% RR7), earning many publishing deal with multiple publishers (AppQuantum, Say Games - the current publisher).

MY CONTRIBUTIONS:

Level design:
  • • Designed, created, balanced and tuning over 100 levels, with over a years of content.
  • • Design monster class, monster behaviors, monster visuals,and wave patterns.
  • • Design and implement "weather system" include of environment weather, elemental system for weapons. Aiming to provide a better strategic depth in gameplay experience.
System design:
  • • Designed and implement the Skill & Buff system, allowing others members to easily create and and new skills without any intervine from developers.
  • • Designed and implement the Drop system, allowing flexible adjustment of item and its drop rate.
General game design:
  • • Designing skill tree features.
  • • Overhaul the UX of the game.
